Key Lime Shake

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1/4 cup 1% milk
  • 2 tablespoons Key lime juice
  • 2 squares graham crackers, divided
  • 2 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons whipped cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon zest and 1 wedge from 1 Key lime

Steps

  1. Add ice cream, milk, key lime juice, 1 graham cracker square, and sweetened condensed milk to the jar of a blender. Blend on high until smooth.
  2. Pour milkshake into a glass; top with whipped cream and zest.
  3. o garnish, break remaining graham square in half, and place one graham into whipped cream, then crumble remaining graham over milkshake.
  4. Cut a lime wedge and tuck into whipped cream.
