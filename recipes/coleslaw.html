Coleslaw

An all america dish. Coleslaw is the perfect companion to any main dish. It is perfect for a hot day and is a great way to get your and your kids to eat their veggies. This recipe is a simple recipe that can be done with the family. It is a great way to get your kids to learn how to cook and to get them to eat their veggies. It is quick, easy and fun to make.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup buttermilk
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 8 cups finely chopped cabbage (approx. 1 head)
  • 1/4 cup shredded carrot (1 medium carrot)
  • 2 tablespoons minced onion

Steps

  1. Be sure cabbage and carrots are chopped up into very fine pieces (a little bigger than rice)
  2. Combine the mayonnaise, sugar, milk, buttermilk, lemon juice, vinegar, salt, and pepper in a large bowl and beat until smooth
  3. Add the cabbage, carrots, and onion, and mix well
  4. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving
